How to Play Monster Survivors

Movement: Use WASD keys or Arrow keys to move your character around the battlefield. Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters. Standing still is extremely dangerous!

Shooting: Click your mouse anywhere on the screen to fire bullets in that direction. Your weapon has unlimited ammunition, so focus on accuracy and positioning rather than conserving shots.

Survival Strategy: Maintain distance from monsters while eliminating them methodically. Use the edges of the arena strategically, but avoid getting cornered. Watch your health bar and try to survive as many waves as possible.

Wave System: After defeating all monsters in a wave, the next wave spawns automatically with more enemies. Each wave increases in difficulty, spawning faster and more numerous monsters. Plan your positioning during the brief pause between waves.
